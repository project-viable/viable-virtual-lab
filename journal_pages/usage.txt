#{h1:Basic usage}#

Underlined text in the journal can be clicked to open a help page for that topic in this panel. For example, try clicking on [url="go_back.txt"]this link[/url]. You can navigate backward and forward in the help page history using the left and right arrow buttons above, respectively.

Certain objects in the scene have help pages associated with them. While hovering or holding an object, press #{prompt:interact_help}# to open its help page.
