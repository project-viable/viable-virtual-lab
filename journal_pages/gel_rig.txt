#{h1:Gel rig}#

Hover over the power supply and press #{prompt:interact_inspect}# to zoom in on it. You must zoom in to use it.

To plug the [url="power_supply.txt"]power supply[/url] into the rig, pick up one end of a wire and attach it to the red socket on the power supply labeled "+", then plug the other end of the same wire into the red "+" socket on the rig. Repeat this with the other wire, connecting the corresponding "-" sockets. It is recommended that you use the red wire to connect the red sockets and the black wire to connect the black sockets, but it is not required.

If the rig is plugged into the power supply and the output is enabled on the power supply but the rig is not receiving current, then you may have forgotten to fill the rig with TAE buffer.
