#{h1:Pipette}#

While holding the pipette, hold down #{prompt:interact_secondary}# or #{prompt:interact_tertiary}# to press the plunger to the first stop. While still holding down the first button, hold down the other button to press the plunger to the second stop.

While holding the pipette, hover over the pipette tip box and press #{prompt:interact_primary}# to attach a tip. To dispose of the tip after using it, hover the pipette over the waste bin and press #{prompt:interact_primary}#.

Bring the tip of the pipette near the top of a pipettable object to automatically zoom in and start pipetting. Pipettable objects include
[ul]
Microcentrifuge tubes
Gel mold (use the pipette to transfer DNA samples to wells in the gel)
[/ul]
